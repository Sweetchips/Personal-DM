About Me

The Man Behind The Madness

Dungeon Master Extraordinaire!

No seriously, I've been doing this crap since I was like 6.

I am a 25 year old aspiring novelist and avid tabletop game player. I have been playing Dungeons and Dragons since about the third grade and I don’t intend on stopping anytime soon!
I live in Burlington, VT with some of my best friends and have a pretty active life of work and fun. In my free time and days off I enjoy playing video games, cooking, going for runs, watching movies and reading a good book. This website is a baby born from nights of watching tv with pen, paper and dice in hand. I’m a creative and no matter what I’m doing in my life, I’m always coming up with stories, characters and settings. Translating those stories into D&D has been an incredibly fulfilling hobby to me and has given fun to many people around me. My life hasn’t been easy to get to this point, but if it weren’t for games like these I’m not sure I’d have made it.
So sit back, download some of these campaigns and take a personal day to play D&D with your friends. You’ve earned it!
