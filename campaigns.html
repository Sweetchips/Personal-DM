The Lords of Castlemire

Journey through the Kingdom of Atlantia... April 20, 2017

A journey to Atlantis! Or Atlantia to be specific. Next up on the list is a campaign centered around a small city with a big role to play in a global conflict. The Lords of Castlemire will place your players in a comfortable yet mysterious world, a world that seems lost in time and centered in an endless ocean. What will start as an easy, fun loving adventure, will quickly and with acceleration, turn into war and destruction. New races, characters, worlds and gods will find hope in the players as they embark to uncover the secrets of Castlemire and the Kingdom of Atlantia.

Heroes of Morton

A Journey Through A World Trapped Inside A Dungeon January 20, 2017

This is Morton. A world somewhere sandiwched in the multiverse, a solid cube of earth, stone, water and life. Originally created as a nursery for three infant gods, Morton has become an ecosystem all within itself. A massive city known as Leigh with railcars and fat cat capialists, a small town full of unique animal people, all sit pretty inside this inescapable cube. However, balance is shifting with the introduction of new people.

Our heroes find themselves taken from their home and brought into the world of Morton with the simple task of collecting three items and using them to escape. Only... this quest is far from simple. Rather it is a journey through a dark world unknown, through the living hearts of people that stand delicately between life and death based off the choices of these new heroes.
